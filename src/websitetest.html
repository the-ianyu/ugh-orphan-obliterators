    About Driveshare

    Driveshare is a rideshare website booker, similar to the Uber app, which incorporates carpooling functions, emission calculations, reward schemes, and accessibility features. Our goal is to provide a reliable and eco-friendly transportation option for everyone.

    Reward Scheme

    Reduce your carbon footprint and save money by sharing your ride with others. Driveshare's carpooling feature matches you with other riders heading in the same direction.

    Driveshare calculates the CO2 emissions saved by carpooling and displays it in your booking details. We believe in transparency and accountability for our environmental impact.

    Earn rewards with every ride you book with Driveshare. Accumulate points and redeem them for discounts on future bookings.
